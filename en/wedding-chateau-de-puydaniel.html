Wedding florist at Château de Puydaniel

Celebrating your wedding at Château de Puydaniel, south of Toulouse? I create fresh, luminous floral decor, in the image of this beautiful pink-brick estate.

Château de Puydaniel and its Toulouse pink-brick facade call for bright, sunny decor. For a wedding held here, I worked a fresh palette of yellow, white and blue touches, in harmony with the stone and the greenery of the grounds.

Bridal bouquet, centrepieces, displays and welcome arrangements: I make sure every detail breathes softness and light, adapting to the summer heat.
