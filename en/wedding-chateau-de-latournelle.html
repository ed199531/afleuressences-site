Wedding florist at Château de Latournelle

A wedding at Château de Latournelle, in the Toulouse area? I compose floral decor that embraces the warm, country spirit of the venue.

Château de Latournelle lends itself wonderfully to colourful, bohemian weddings. Under the marquee or in the vaulted rooms, I love to unfold generous compositions blending seasonal flowers, textures and vivid colours.

Large standing floral pieces, centrepieces in coloured glass, country-style bouquets: every element is designed to create a joyful, natural atmosphere, true to your story.
