Fine Art Weddings

Anne-Marie Roig, floral designer in L'Union, creates Fine Art compositions for exceptional weddings in Toulouse and the surrounding area.

Artistic bouquets

Rooted in photography, Fine Art Floral brings a sense of detail, timeless refined elegance, and lightness. Every creation is a unique piece inspired by Flemish paintings and English gardens.

Each composition brings together a wide variety of flowers and textures. Visual lightness, colour work and three-dimensional movement are the core principles of this technique.

The story behind the design shapes an impression of abundance, with natural, unstructured forms. I have been training in these Floral Designer methods for two years.

My wish is to offer you unique floral compositions inspired by romantic and natural abundance. Depending on your story, the floral décor will be imagined to reflect you, whether in the bridal bouquet, table centrepieces, floral jewellery, arches, or larger installations. I'm available to design the floral scenography of your wedding.

“Cendrillon”, a shoot immersion

Yes, a technical visit of the ceremony and reception venue is arranged with you beforehand. On the day, I deliver and install all the decor on site.

It depends on the scale of the décor desired, the budget is always defined together at the first meeting.
