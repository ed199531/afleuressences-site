Weddings

Wedding florist in L'Union and Toulouse, the most beautiful day of your life.

An exceptional atmosphere for an exceptional day: your wedding!

Themes, colours, budgets respected

I'll advise you based on your preferences, themes, colours, dreams, without forgetting your budget, so your wedding truly reflects your personalities and stays in your memory as a radiant day.

Advice, listening to your needs

  • Decorations for the ceremony venue, reception venue and cars
  • Floral jewellery: crowns, bracelets, boutonnieres, combs, headpieces
