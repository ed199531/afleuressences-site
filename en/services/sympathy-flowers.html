Sympathy flowers

Flowers make it possible to express a tribute, affection or gratitude towards the person who has passed. I guide you in choosing floral arrangements suited to your wishes and to the ceremony.

Personal guidance

Every tribute is unique. During a conversation, I take the time to advise you on the choice of flowers, colours and composition, taking into account your wishes, the personality of the departed, the type of ceremony and your budget.

Floral creations to express your emotions

Flowers accompany the final moments and carry a message of love, respect and gratitude. I create arrangements designed with care, to pay a sincere and delicate tribute.

Creations for every tribute

  • Floral wreaths and hearts
  • Cushions and funeral sprays
  • Crosses and casket arrangements
  • Casket tops and fronts
  • Plant arrangements in bowls or baskets

Arrangements can be delivered at your convenience to the funeral home, to your home or to the place of the funeral, in L'Union, Toulouse and the surrounding area.

Grave flowering and upkeep

I also offer a grave flowering and upkeep service, with formulas adapted to your needs: flower delivery, renewal of arrangements and regular care of the grave when you cannot tend to it yourself.

This grave flowering subscription starts at €30 per month and covers the cemeteries of L'Union, Saint-Jean, Castelmaurou and Croix-Daurade (Toulouse). An annual subscription is available, with a quote based on the schedule of visits and your flowering choices.
