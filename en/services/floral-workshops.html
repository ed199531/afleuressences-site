Floral workshops

A floral workshop with everything you need to create, imagine and make, it's possible! Just set aside some time with friends, family or colleagues, pick a date, and choose your programme, of course depending on the season.

You'll leave with your own creation. Personalised classes at home or at the Afleuressences studio, in L'Union.

  • Price: from €45/person (flowers included) for adult workshops, €20 for children's workshops
  • Duration: 1h30 to 2h for adults, 1h to 1h30 for children
  • Location: at home or at the Afleuressences studio in L'Union
  • Booking: by appointment, at least one week in advance
