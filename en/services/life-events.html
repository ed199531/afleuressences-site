Life events

Flowers for births, christenings and birthdays in L'Union and Toulouse.

A calendar full of occasions

Throughout the seasons and the calendar of celebrations, there are countless opportunities to bring joy and give flowers. Because they bring colour and good spirits into our lives, I create bouquets and floral compositions to order, respecting the flowers and plants of our region, between L'Union and Toulouse, and the rhythm of the seasons.

Designs to suit your budget

I offer designs adapted to your budget for every life event.

  • Births, christenings, communions, birthdays, family celebrations
  • Hen parties
  • Calendar celebrations: Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, All Saints' Day, Christmas, Easter, May Day, International Women's Rights Day, New Year's Day…
  • Decorative, structured or loose bouquets, contemporary or rustic styles, floral crowns, pin compositions, table centrepieces, potted plant arrangements
