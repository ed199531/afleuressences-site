Wedding florist at Château de Merville

Getting married at Château de Merville, north of Toulouse? I create bespoke floral designs worthy of this exceptional venue.

Château de Merville, with its French formal gardens and boxwood mazes, is a rare setting for a wedding. I love composing decor that converses with the architecture and the light of the grounds, without ever overwhelming them.

For Margot and Hamza's wedding, I imagined a warm palette of orange and golden tones: an abundant bridal bouquet, buttonholes, centrepieces and floral crowns, in harmony with the château's stone and woodwork.
