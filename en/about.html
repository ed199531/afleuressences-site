About me

Anne-Marie Roig, artisan florist in L'Union, near Toulouse.

Greenery, plants

From childhood to the age of twenty, I lived in Africa, in Gabon, surrounded by lush vegetation. Florist should have been my first choice in life, but things turned out differently.

A passion for flowers

My passion for flowers isn't recent, but I set it aside for many years.

Creativity

I started expressing my creativity 8 years ago, taking floral art classes at the MJC in L'Union.

Floral work

Over the years, through my creations, I came to accept the idea of pursuing this as a profession. Only one thing was missing to be credible in the eyes of my future clients: a florist's diploma.

Specialised artisan

Thanks to my sister and the opportunity of a training leave, I was able to complete a florist's certification (CAP) through work-study at the CFA in Blagnac and at Aubépine in Toulouse.

A floral studio near Toulouse

Creating my studio, Afleuressences, is the fulfilment of my dream. Thank you to my family for supporting me in this project.

Where there's a will, there's a way!
